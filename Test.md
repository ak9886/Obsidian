#flashcards

Iteration formula for Newton–Raphson method:: $$\huge{x_{n+1} = x_n - \frac{f(x_n)}{f'(x_n)}}$$
Trapezoidal rule formula:: $$\huge{\frac{h}{2}\left[f_0 + 2\sum_{i=1}^{n-1} f_i + f_n\right]}$$
Simpson’s one-third rule formula:: $$\huge{\frac{h}{3}\left[f_0 + 4\sum_{\text{odd}} f_i + 2\sum_{\text{even}} f_i + f_n\right]}$$
