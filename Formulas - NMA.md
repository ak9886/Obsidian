#Maths 
$$
f(x) = f(a) + f'(a)(x-a) + \frac{f''(a)}{2!}(x-a)^2 + \cdots + \frac{f^{(n)}(a)}{n!}(x-a)^n + R_n(x)
$$

where the remainder $R_n(x)$ can be expressed in Lagrange form as:

$$
R_n(x) = \frac{f^{(n+1)}(\xi)}{(n+1)!}(x-a)^{n+1}
$$

for some $\xi$ between $a$ and $x$.
