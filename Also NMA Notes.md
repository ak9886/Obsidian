#Maths 
1. Khan Academy  
   - Channel: [Khan Academy](https://www.youtube.com/user/khanacademy)  
   - Well-known for clear and concise math tutorials including Calculus and basic numerical methods concepts.

2. MIT OpenCourseWare  
   - Playlist: [MIT 18.01 Single Variable Calculus, Fall 2006](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL221E2BBF13BECF6C)  
   - Detailed university-level calculus and numerical methods lectures.

3. PatrickJMT  
   - Channel: [patrickJMT](https://www.youtube.com/user/patrickJMT)  
   - High-quality videos on linear algebra, calculus, differential equations, and numerical methods like Gauss-Seidel, Runge-Kutta, and more.

4. 3Blue1Brown  
   - Channel: [3Blue1Brown](https://www.youtube.com/c/3blue1brown)  
   - Visually intuitive explanations of math concepts including some numerical methods topics.

5. Alex Maths Engineering  
   - Channel: [Alex Maths Engineering](https://www.youtube.com/c/AlexMathsEngineering)  
   - Focused on engineering mathematics, including differential calculus and numerical methods.

6. MySecretMathTutor  
   - Channel: [MySecretMathTutor](https://www.youtube.com/c/MySecretMathTutor)  
   - Covers a broad range of math topics including step-by-step numerical methods tutorials.

7. Numerical Analysis Full Course (Part 1)  
   - Video: [Numerical Analysis Full Course | Part 1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jvuBnc8C6Js)  
   - In-depth course covering many numerical methods topics.

For a comprehensive study, these channels provide English tutorials ranging from basics to advanced numerical methods and related engineering mathematics topics. They are well-rated for their clarity, examples, and teaching styles.

This selection should meet your preference for English-language instruction on all the topics you listed.
